About | Marc Steven de Vera

Snapshot.msd is a premier photography service dedicated to capturing life's most precious moments. A low-cost skilled photographer at a very affordable price ranges, I ensure that every shot tells a story. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or a simple family portrait, I bring creativity and professionalism to every project.

My mission is to provide high-quality, timeless images that you can cherish for a lifetime. I believe in the power of photography to preserve memories and evoke emotions. Let me help you create lasting impressions with our exceptional photography services.

I wanna capture moments of laughter, struggles,events, and most especially, moments that can last forever. My passion to photography started ever since I was young, I got the chance to pursue photography after I worked on summer to have the money to buy a DSLR in which I still use today. I have a deep knowledge about photography. I applied my knowledge and skills on tasked that allowed me to use them. I enjoy anything about media and I would like to share my keen knowledge and experience while working with y'all. ​
